    The Author

    Kim Pezzeminti sees threads that others miss. Connections between disciplines that normally wouldn't intersect. People who don't yet know they need each other. She's been doing this her whole life, from the tile industry to a ranch in Wyoming, and now with her Gypsy Vanners at the center of it all. Kim facilitates experiences; she brings together the right horses, the right people, the right places of convergence and connection, and lets the rest unfold.
